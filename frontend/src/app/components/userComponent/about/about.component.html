ABOUT DR.CARE

When you need answers, you know where to go.

Located in the vibrant heart of India, Dr.care Hospitals is one of the largest private multi-specialist hospitals in Asia. Mata Amritanandamayi Devi laid the hospital's cornerstone in Kochi in 1998. We are dedicated to providing the most sophisticated healthcare to people from all walks of life.

PRIMARY CARE

Comprehensive, continuous care for individuals and families with an emphasis on prevention and wellness.

LAB TEST

Accurate, timely laboratory diagnostics to support informed clinical decisions and effective treatment plans.

SYMPTOM CHECK

Advanced tools and expert consultations to assess symptoms and guide patients to the right care pathway.

HEART RATE

Real-time heart monitoring and cardiovascular assessments for proactive heart health management.
